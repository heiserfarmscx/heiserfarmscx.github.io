2021 Heiser Farms Cyclocross

Saturday, October 2nd, 2021

Course Description:
Heiser Farms, located in Dayton, Oregon, has quickly become a favorite cyclocross venue. The farm offers a montage of terrain, the famous shoe stealing BOG, stunning vistas of the Willamette Valley and family farm fun. We will be following Oregon's outdoor mask mandate protocol.
